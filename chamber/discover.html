About Utah County

Population (approximate): 685,000

Utah County Size 2,142 sq. milea

Population Density 258 individuals per square mile

Valley Elevations 4,480 - 4,700 ft above sea level

Highest Peak Mt. Nebo at 11,928 ft above sea level

Origins and Name

The name "Utah" is a nod to the region's indigenous roots. Derived from the Native American "Ute" tribe, it translates to 'people of the mountains'.
